# Avatar Video — Download Instructions

Download a FREE stock video of a person teaching/explaining and save it here as `teacher-talking.mp4`.

## Best Free Sources (no attribution needed):

### Option 1: Pexels (recommended)
Go to https://www.pexels.com/search/videos/man%20explaining/ and download any video showing a person talking/gesturing. Filter by "Portrait" orientation for best fit in the circular avatar.

Good search terms:
- "man explaining"
- "teacher talking"
- "presenter speaking"
- "business man talking"

### Option 2: Pixabay
Go to https://pixabay.com/videos/search/man%20talking/ — all free, no attribution.

### Requirements:
- Duration: 30+ seconds (it will loop automatically)
- Format: MP4
- Orientation: Portrait or square preferred (gets cropped to circle)
- Content: Person talking, with hand gestures if possible
- Resolution: Any (gets scaled to 220x220px circle)

### After downloading:
Save as: `public/video/teacher-talking.mp4`
The AvatarBubble component will automatically use it with `<OffthreadVideo>`.
